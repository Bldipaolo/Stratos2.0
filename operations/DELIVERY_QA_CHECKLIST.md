# Stratos Delivery QA Checklist

## Website/front-end
- Mobile hero clearly says who it is for, what outcome, and next action.
- Click-to-call works on mobile.
- Primary form submits successfully.
- Sticky CTA does not cover important content.
- Page speed is acceptable for mobile sales use.
- No placeholder lorem ipsum or dummy testimonials.

## Automation/n8n
- Trigger fires from the real form/phone/source.
- Data is normalized into the expected fields.
- Staff notification arrives with enough context to act.
- Customer message has correct merge fields.
- Duplicate/throttle rules prevent spam.
- Failure path alerts the owner or Stratos.
- No credentials or secrets are committed.

## Proof and claims
- Demo/projection/client result labels are clear.
- No fake revenue, fake reviews, or fake client claims.
- Screenshots are dated and tied to source/context.
- Client approval captured before public case-study usage.

## Handoff
- Client knows where leads go.
- Client knows who responds.
- Client knows how to pause/disable automation.
- Stratos has weekly reporting cadence set.
