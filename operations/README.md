# Stratos Operations Library

This is the logistical backbone for selling and delivering Stratos without custom chaos.

## Use this order

1. `CLIENT_ONBOARDING_CHECKLIST.md` — collect the business facts.
2. `templates/client-intake-snapshot.md` — summarize discovery in one page.
3. `templates/automation-scope-sheet.md` — define exactly what the automation does.
4. `DELIVERY_QA_CHECKLIST.md` — test before launch.
5. `WEEKLY_OPERATOR_CADENCE.md` — retain and expand monthly value.

## Operating principle

A Stratos client should never feel like they bought “AI.” They should feel that their business became easier to buy from, easier to operate, and easier to measure.
