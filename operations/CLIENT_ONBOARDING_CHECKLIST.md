# Client Onboarding Checklist

## Business basics
- Legal business name
- Public brand name
- Primary location(s)
- Main phone number
- Main booking/contact email
- Website and important landing pages
- Google Business Profile URL
- Social links

## Offer and economics
- Top 3 services by revenue
- Average customer/patient/case value
- Current monthly lead volume
- Current close/book rate estimate
- Most profitable customer type
- Services to avoid promoting

## Lead flow
- What happens after a form fill?
- What happens after a missed call?
- Who owns follow-up?
- What systems are used now? CRM, phone, booking, EHR, dispatch, email, SMS.
- What breaks when the team is busy?

## Proof and compliance
- Approved reviews/testimonials
- Before/after assets, if allowed
- Regulated language restrictions
- Claims that must not be made
- Required disclaimers

## Launch assets
- Logo files
- Brand colors/fonts if available
- Staff photos or facility photos
- Service descriptions
- FAQs
- Tracking/analytics access

## Decision checklist
- Primary business outcome
- Recommended Stratos package
- First automation to launch
- Success metric for first 30 days
- Owner of client approvals
