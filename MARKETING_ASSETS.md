# Stratos AI Marketing Assets

This repo includes the premium Instagram month pack for Stratos AI.

## Instagram Month Pack V2

Path: `marketing-assets/instagram-month-pack-v2/`

Packaged archive: `marketing-assets/instagram-month-pack-v2.zip`

Includes:

- 30 feed posts, 1080x1080 PNG
- 12 stories, 1080x1920 PNG
- 20 carousel slides
- 6 vertical reel/video assets, 1080x1920 MP4
- Premium Higgsfield-generated hero image
- Captions
- 30-day content calendar
- Video scripts
- Preview contact sheet
- Manifest

## Notes

- Stratos logo/type is composited locally for brand consistency and clean text.
- Higgsfield generation was credit-limited after one premium GPT Image 2 hero image.
- OpenAI/FAL image backend was unavailable in the current Hermes environment at generation time, so no OpenAI-generated image assets are included in this pack.
- Source/refinement script: `scripts/marketing/refine_instagram_v2.py`.
